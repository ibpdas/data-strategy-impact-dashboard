How to read this dashboard

ValueLine guide, notes and model explanation.

How the ValueLine Framework Works

ValueLine: Data to Impact Navigator is a learning and reflection tool showcasing a three level causal model for public sector data leaders. It illustrates how Level 1 foundational enablers, Level 2 organisational maturity and Level 3 outcomes connect to demonstrate data to impact pathways. This is not a benchmark. It supports structured conversations, early planning and clearer articulation of how data foundations contribute to public value.

Understanding the three levels

Level 1 foundations are the conditions directly under organisational control. They include strategy, leadership, standards, community and engagement. They are leading indicators of success and tend to move fastest.

Level 2 maturity represents how data is used in practice across uses, data, leadership, culture, tools and skills. It emerges from Level 1 foundations and evolves more slowly.

Level 3 outcomes represent public value: savings, satisfaction, efficiency, time saved and system contributions. They are lagging indicators and take longest to materialise.

Indicator types

  • Leading indicators provide early signals of enabling conditions at Level 1.
  • Signal indicators reflect developing system behaviours and adoption at Level 2.
  • Lagging indicators reflect realised benefits and outcomes at Level 3.

Methodology and evidence sources

  • Delivery artefacts, trackers and governance papers inform Level 1.
  • Annual maturity assessments, surveys and engagement insight inform Level 2.
  • Verified benefit cases, reuse metrics and public value indicators inform Level 3.

Caveats and intended use

This dashboard simplifies complex organisational dynamics. It is designed for strategic discussion and early planning, not formal assurance or audit. Performance data is illustrative and does not represent any real organisation. The model assumes causality flows forward from foundations to outcomes and backward from desired outcomes to future foundation design.
